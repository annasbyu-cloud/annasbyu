Why I Love Climbing

Rock climbing is more than just a sport — it's a mental and physical challenge that pushes you to your limits in the best way possible. Whether it's bouldering at the gym or climbing outdoors in the Utah mountains, I love every second of it. There's nothing quite like reaching the top of a route you've been working on for weeks!

Types of Climbing

There are several different styles of climbing, each with its own unique challenge:

  1. Bouldering
    • No ropes or harness needed
    • Short, powerful routes called "problems"
    • Great for beginners
  2. Top Rope Climbing
    • Rope anchored at the top of the wall
    • Very safe and beginner friendly
    • Common at indoor gyms
  3. Lead Climbing
    • Climber clips the rope as they go up
    • More advanced technique required
    • Used in competitions
  4. Outdoor / Trad Climbing
    • Climbing real rock faces outside
    • Requires gear placement knowledge
    • Utah has incredible outdoor routes!

Beginner Tips 🏔️

Just getting started with climbing? Here are my top tips:

  • Start with bouldering — no gear needed and super fun
  • Focus on your footwork, not just your arms
  • Rest between attempts — climbing is very taxing on your fingers
  • Visit a local gym like Momentum Climbing in Salt Lake City
  • Don't be afraid to ask more experienced climbers for beta (advice)
  • Invest in a good pair of climbing shoes early on
