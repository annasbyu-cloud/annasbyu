About Me

A little bit about who I am

Hi, I'm Anna 👋

I'm a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University with an emphasis in Accounting, originally from American Fork, Utah. I have a passion for the outdoors — whether that's hiking, climbing, or just soaking up some fresh air in the mountains.

When I'm not studying or working as a licensed Master Esthetician, you'll probably find me singing or performing — music has always been a big part of my life. I also love to travel and have been lucky enough to experience life in Bosnia, Belgium, Croatia, and Australia. Oh, and one fun fact: I'm a twin! 🎉

I'm driven by a love of learning, a commitment to excellence (4.0 GPA and counting!), and a genuine desire to connect with and serve the people around me.
